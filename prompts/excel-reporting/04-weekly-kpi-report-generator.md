# Weekly KPI Report Generator

## Use Case

Use this prompt when you need to create clear, executive-level weekly KPI reports from operational or business data.

## Prompt

You are an expert business reporting consultant.

Help me create a professional weekly KPI report.

Business context:
[Describe the business or project]

Audience:
[Executives / Managers / Team Leads]

KPIs:
[List the indicators]

Current performance:
[Paste values or summary]

Challenges/issues:
[Explain blockers or concerns]

Requirements:

* Write an executive summary.
* Highlight wins and risks.
* Identify trends or anomalies.
* Suggest actions for improvement.
* Make the report concise and professional.

## Expected Output

A structured executive KPI report with insights, risks, recommendations, and a leadership-friendly summary.

## Pro Tip

Include historical values from previous weeks to help identify meaningful trends.
