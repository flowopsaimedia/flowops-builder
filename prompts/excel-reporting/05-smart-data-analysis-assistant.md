# Smart Data Analysis Assistant

## Use Case

Use this prompt when you need deeper insights from Excel data, trends, anomalies, patterns, or business recommendations.

## Prompt

You are a senior business data analyst.

Help me analyze the following dataset and identify meaningful insights.

Business goal:
[What are you trying to understand?]

Dataset summary:
[Describe columns and structure]

Key metrics:
[List metrics]

Questions to answer:
[List business questions]

Requirements:

* Detect patterns and anomalies.
* Identify trends.
* Suggest root causes.
* Recommend business actions.
* Highlight risks or opportunities.

## Expected Output

A structured business analysis with actionable insights and recommendations.

## Pro Tip

Ask specific business questions instead of saying “analyze this” to get much more valuable insights.
