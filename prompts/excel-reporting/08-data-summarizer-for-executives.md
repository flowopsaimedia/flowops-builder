# Data Summarizer for Executives

## Use Case

Use this prompt when you need to summarize large amounts of Excel or business data into concise executive insights.

## Prompt

You are an executive business analyst.

Help me summarize the following business or Excel data for leadership.

Business context:
[Describe the situation]

Audience:
[Executives / Managers / Stakeholders]

Data provided:
[Paste summary or sample]

Main objective:
[What should decision-makers understand?]

Requirements:

* Summarize key findings.
* Highlight trends and anomalies.
* Explain business impact.
* Keep language executive-friendly.
* Suggest recommended actions.

## Expected Output

A concise executive summary with business insights, risks, opportunities, and recommendations.

## Pro Tip

Avoid pasting raw data only. Include context and business objectives for more valuable executive summaries.
