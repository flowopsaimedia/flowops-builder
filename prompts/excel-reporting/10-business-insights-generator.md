# Business Insights Generator

## Use Case

Use this prompt when you need strategic insights from business, operational, or Excel data to support decision-making.

## Prompt

You are a senior business strategy analyst.

Help me generate meaningful business insights from the following information.

Business context:
[Describe the business]

Objective:
[What are we trying to improve or understand?]

Current metrics:
[List KPIs or indicators]

Observed concerns:
[Issues or uncertainties]

Data summary:
[Paste relevant information]

Requirements:

* Identify meaningful insights.
* Detect opportunities and risks.
* Recommend practical actions.
* Prioritize business impact.
* Suggest follow-up analysis.

## Expected Output

A business-oriented analysis with practical recommendations and prioritized insights.

## Pro Tip

The better the business context, the smarter and more useful the insights will be.
